Media registration

Please review the documentation requirements.

Photo of yourself

The photo you supply will be printed on your access badge as is.

  • Recent passport-style photo
  • Only the following formats are accepted .jpg, jpeg or gif
  • Maximum file size of 2 MB
  • The photographs must show the full front view of the head, with the face in the middle of the image
  • Pale or white background
  • No hats or sunglasses
  • Photo must be in colour
  • A photograph of your drivers license, passport or any other picture id will not be accepted

Valid government-issued ID with photo

Acceptable forms of ID

  • Canadian citizens: Driver's License, health card or Passport
  • Non-Canadian citizens: Passport or permanent resident card only

Letter of assignment

  • Only the following formats are accepted .pdf, .jpg, jpeg or gif
  • The letter must name the applicant and his/her role within the organization
  • The letter must indicate that the applicant's assignment covers the 2017 Royal Tour
  • The letter must be written on official letterhead and be signed by an authorized individual and include that person's contact details

Valid media credential

  • Only the following formats are accepted .pdf, .jpg, jpeg or gif
  • The media credential must be issued by a recognized professional body
  • A valid passport may be provided in lieu of a media credential
